Candidates for the HST Rolling Snapshots Program from ZTF Supernova

Selection Criteria

Candidates must be: classified as SN by ZTF, within 150 Mpc, and younger than ~3 weeks (the filter specifically uses age<14 days). Another criterion is that the source should be brighter than 19.5 apparent magnitude (mpeak<19.5), but early testing suggests that sources classified while young are likely to meet this cut even if no magnitude limit is specified.
Here, age is defined as the time since the midpoint between the first detection and last non-detection, or just time since first detection if there were no previous non-detections.
Candidates are considered from both the CLU and RCF programs.
